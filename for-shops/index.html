What we handle, what you keep

We handle

  • Trip planning and itinerary design, built around your shop's customers and calendar
  • Supplier coordination (resorts, liveaboards, transfers)
  • Customer payment processing and accounting
  • Co-branded marketing materials drafted for your shop
  • Pre-trip prep, on-trip logistics, post-trip recap
  • Incident coordination across weather, supplier, and logistics disruptions

You keep

  • The customer relationship and contact list
  • Ownership of all customer-facing channels (email list, store, social media)
  • Choice of who hosts each trip on site
  • 100% of post-trip gear, training, and service revenue
  • Authority over the trip calendar and direction
  • Full ownership of the customer record, always
Your choice per trip

Three ways we can run a trip together

All modes are co-branded with your shop. Dive operations are always run by the resort or liveaboard's own divemasters and instructors. Dorado is the host and logistics layer, not the dive operator.

1

You host

You travel with the group as the trip host. We've done the planning, supplier work, and customer logistics ahead of the trip. We're on call throughout but not on site. You stay the face of the trip with your customers, and we handle everything behind it.

2

Co-hosted

We're both on the trip. You stay the face of the trip; we handle the operations and anything that comes up on site: airport meets, transfers, and day-to-day logistics. Useful when the shop wants the customer relationship but not the operational load on site.

3

Dorado hosts

Dorado travels with the group as the on-site host, co-branded with your shop. Useful when you can't travel but the shop wants the trip to run.

How the math works

No cost to your shop. Commission comes from the operator.

Customers pay the trip price. The operator pays a commission on the booking.

Of the commission, your shop's share is 30% when you or your staff are on the trip, and 25% when Dorado runs it end to end. On top of that, trips you host include a comped trip-host spot, negotiated with the supplier per group thresholds.

And the rest, the gear, training, and service revenue your customers spend at your shop after they get back, is yours, untouched.

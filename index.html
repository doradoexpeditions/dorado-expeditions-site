How it works

What Dorado is, in three lines.

01

Operator

We hold the resort and liveaboard operator relationships that make group trips work, so you don't have to build or manage them.

02

Trip host

Your choice per trip: you host, we co-host, or Dorado hosts. Dive operations are always run by the resort or liveaboard's own divemasters.

03

Shop partner

Co-branded. Your shop owns the customer record. You keep the gear and training revenue. Written non-circumvention in every partnership agreement.
